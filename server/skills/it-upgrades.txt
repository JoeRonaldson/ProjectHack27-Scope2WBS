IT Upgrades Planning Skill

Use this guidance when the project scope is primarily IT upgrades, infrastructure refreshes, platform migrations, or enterprise technology rollout.

Planning priorities:
- Break work into discovery, design, build/configure, test, deployment, and hypercare phases.
- Distinguish environment layers (network, compute, storage, application, endpoint, identity, security).
- Include integration touchpoints, dependency mapping, and compatibility checks.
- Add cutover and rollback planning activities for production-impacting changes.
- Include data migration/validation steps when systems or schemas change.
- Capture user readiness, training, and service desk handover activities.

Risk and controls emphasis:
- Business continuity and downtime minimization.
- Security hardening, access controls, and compliance checks.
- Performance and capacity validation before go-live.
- Change management approvals and stakeholder communications.

Output expectations:
- Use precise IT delivery terminology.
- Group tasks by platform/workstream while keeping end-to-end sequence clear.
- Avoid civil/building tasks unless explicitly requested in scope.
